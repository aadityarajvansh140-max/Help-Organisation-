About Help Organisation

Help Organisation is a registered non-profit organisation (Reg. No. 1166) based in Rajmahal, Sahibganj, Jharkhand. Since 2010, the organisation has been working to support persons with disabilities through mobility assistance, awareness programmes and community support. Every wheelchair distributed brings greater independence, confidence and hope to individuals and families.
